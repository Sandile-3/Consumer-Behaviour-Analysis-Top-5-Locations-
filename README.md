# Consumer-Behaviour-Analysis-Top-5-Locations-
Consumer Behaviour Data Analysis – Top 5 Locations
Project Overview

This project analyzes shopping behavior across the top 5 customer locations in the United States. It investigates trends and patterns in purchases, including product preferences, sizes, colors, seasonal behavior, and demographic insights, to support data-driven decision-making and optimize business strategies.

Objectives

Identify the top-performing products, categories, and colors in each location.

Understand customer demographics and purchase frequency patterns.

Analyze seasonal and gender-based purchase trends.

Provide actionable insights for marketing, inventory management, and sales optimization.

Data

The analysis uses customer transaction data, including:

Location – Top 5 customer locations

Gender – Male or Female

Age – Customer age

Item Purchased – Product purchased

Size & Color – Size and color preferences

Season – Time of purchase

Purchase Frequency – Weekly, monthly, quarterly, annually, etc.

Key Insights

Medium-sized clothing items dominate purchases across locations.

Women generally make more frequent purchases, while male purchases spike seasonally.

Clothing and accessories are the top-performing categories.

Regional trends indicate varying top-selling products, colors, and seasonal behavior.

Predictive analysis can forecast likely purchases based on gender, location, and season.

Visualizations

The project includes the following dashboards:

General Dashboard: Customer demographics, average spend, and ratings.

Product & Category Performance: Top products and categories by location.

Seasonal Trends: Purchases by season and gender.

Frequency Analysis: Purchase frequency by gender and location.

Size & Color Preferences: Preferences by location and gender.
